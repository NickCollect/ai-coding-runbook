GPT-5.4 Mini brings good performance to cost-sensitive workflows.

## Strengths

- Over 2x faster than GPT-5 Mini. Low latency makes it responsive for interactive coding and agentic loops.
- Reliable tool use. Handles multi-step agent workflows, subagent delegation, and screenshot-based computer use.
- Strong multimodal understanding. Processes images and screenshots well for its size.

## Limitations

- Not a frontier model. For more difficult problems where peak intelligence matters, [GPT-5.4](https://cursor.com/docs/models/gpt-5-4.md) is the better pick.
- Smaller capacity for long, open-ended reasoning chains compared to the full-size model.
