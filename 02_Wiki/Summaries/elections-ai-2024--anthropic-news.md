Dec 12, 2024 — Anthropic's election-cycle 2024 retrospective. Election-related activity <0.5% of overall Claude use; ticked up to ~1% in weeks before US election. Approximately 100 election-related enforcement actions globally (warnings + some bans for repeat violators).

**Three-part safety approach.**
1. Comprehensive policies — May 2024 expansion to address influence campaigns, voter targeting, impersonation, election interference.
2. Rigorous policy vulnerability testing — 12+ rounds with external policy experts. Daily testing during US cycle. Tested ahead of India, South Africa, Mexico, UK, France, EU Parliamentary elections.
3. Authoritative-source redirects — TurboVote/Democracy Works (US), EU Parliament site, UK Electoral Commission, France elections website.

Chatbot architecture (1:1 interactions, no algorithmic feed amplification, text-only — no deepfakes) lowers abuse vectors vs social media. Used Clio for usage analysis.
