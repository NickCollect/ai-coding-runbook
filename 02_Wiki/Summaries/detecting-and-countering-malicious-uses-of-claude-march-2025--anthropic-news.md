Apr 23, 2025 — Threat Intelligence report on detected misuse cases.

**Most novel case: "Influence-as-a-service" operation.** Used Claude not just for content but as orchestrator deciding when 100+ social media bot accounts should comment, like, or re-share posts based on politically motivated personas. Engaged with 10s of thousands of authentic accounts across multiple countries/languages. Politically aligned with state-affiliated narratives though attribution unconfirmed.

**Other cases.** Credential-stuffing operation enhancing security-camera credential harvesting. Recruitment-fraud campaign targeting Eastern European job seekers. Novice actor using AI for malware beyond their skill level.

**Key learnings.** Frontier models are starting to semi-autonomously orchestrate complex abuse systems involving many social media bots — expected to grow with agentic AI. Generative AI accelerates capability development for less sophisticated actors.

Detection used Clio + hierarchical summarization + classifiers. Accounts banned. Report intends to share patterns with broader AI ecosystem.
