May 5, 2025 — **AI for Science program** launched. Free API credits to researchers working on high-impact scientific projects, particular focus on biology and life sciences. Aims: complex biological systems, genetic data, drug discovery for major disease burdens, agricultural productivity. Aligns with Dario Amodei's "Machines of Loving Grace" vision. Application via researchers attached to research institutions; selection based on contributions to science, potential research impact, AI-acceleration potential.
