Dec 18, 2025 — Safeguards-team measures for Claude handling emotional-support / suicide-self-harm / sycophancy. Three areas:

**Suicide and self-harm.** System prompt guidance for sensitive conversations (publicly available). RLHF training shaped by human-preference data + Anthropic-defined ideal-character data + in-house experts. Product: classifier scans active conversations for suicidal ideation or fictional self-harm scenarios; **crisis banner** appears with country-specific helplines from **ThroughLine** (verified global network across 170+ countries — 988 in US/Canada, Samaritans in UK, Life Link in Japan).

**Sycophancy reduction.** Effort to reduce tendency to tell users what they want to hear vs what's true and helpful — described in fuller post.

**Age requirement.** Claude requires users to be 18+.
