Apr 9, 2024 — Anthropic Societal Impacts study measuring **model persuasiveness across generations**. Method: 3-step (rate agreement → see persuasive argument → re-rate). Studied Claude generations 1, 2, 3 across compact and frontier model classes; topics chosen were complex/emerging issues (online content moderation, space-exploration ethics, AI-content use) where opinions are less hardened.

**Headline result.** Each successive Claude generation rated more persuasive than prior. **Claude 3 Opus produces arguments not statistically different in persuasiveness from human-written arguments.** 28 topics × 56 claims dataset released on HuggingFace.

Important for tracking AI risk: persuasion ties to disinformation generation, manipulation, and acting against user interests.
