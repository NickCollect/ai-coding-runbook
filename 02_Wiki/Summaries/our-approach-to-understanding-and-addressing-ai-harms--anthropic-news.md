Apr 21, 2025 — Anthropic's framework for assessing AI harms beyond catastrophic-risk-focused RSP. **Five baseline impact dimensions**: physical, psychological, economic, societal, individual autonomy. For each: likelihood, scale, affected populations, duration, causality, technology contribution, mitigation feasibility. Used by Safeguards team for proportional response (Usage Policy, evaluations, detection, enforcement from prompt mods to account blocking). Complements RSP — RSP for catastrophic, this framework for full spectrum. Acknowledged as evolving.
