Mar 19, 2025 — Frontier Red Team report on national-security capability trajectory across four model releases. Models displaying "early warning" signs of rapid progress in dual-use capabilities — approaching/exceeding undergraduate cybersecurity skill, expert-level biology knowledge in some areas. Present-day models still fall short of substantially-elevated-national-security-risk thresholds.

**Cybersecurity 2024: a "zero to one" moment.** On Intercode CTF (high-school designed), Claude went from <25% to nearly all challenges in <1 year. On Cybench (more advanced), Claude 3.7 Sonnet solves ~33% in 5 attempts vs ~5% a year earlier. Improvements across pwn, web, crypto categories. Still lags expert humans in reverse-engineering binaries and network reconnaissance/exploitation without help. Confidence in real improvement built via custom CTFs (not in training data). Detail on bio capabilities also covered.
