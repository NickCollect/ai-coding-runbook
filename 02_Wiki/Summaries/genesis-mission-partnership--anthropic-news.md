Dec 18, 2025 — Multi-year **Anthropic + US DOE Genesis Mission** partnership. Three domains: American energy dominance, biological/life sciences, scientific productivity. Potential to affect work at all 17 US national labs.

**Anthropic provides** DOE researchers with: Claude access + a team of Anthropic engineers building purpose-built tools — agents for DOE's highest-priority challenges, MCP servers connecting Claude to scientific instruments and tools, Claude Skills for specialized expertise on relevant scientific workflows.
