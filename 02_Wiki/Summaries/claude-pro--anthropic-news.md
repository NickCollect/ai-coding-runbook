Sep 7, 2023 — **Claude Pro** launched as the first paid plan for the claude.ai chat experience (US/UK). $20/month US, £18/month UK. Provides 5× the usage of the free tier (more messages), priority access during high traffic, and early access to new features. Powered by Claude 2.

The release marks Anthropic's transition from free-only consumer access to a tiered consumer subscription model that later evolved into Pro/Max/Team/Enterprise.
