Feb 29, 2024 — Practical post on prompt engineering for businesses building with Claude. Premise: good prompts improve outputs, reduce deployment costs, ensure customer-facing experiences are on-brand.

Case study: Anthropic's prompt-engineering team helped a Fortune 500 company build a customer-facing chat assistant powered by Claude that answers complex questions quickly and accurately. Benefits of effective prompts: accuracy (reduces hallucinations beyond Claude's baseline), reliability, brand-aligned results, processing efficiency for tasks like contract review, customer Q&A, data processing. The post is a representative early example of Anthropic's enterprise prompt-engineering guidance.
