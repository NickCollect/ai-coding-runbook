Jan 15, 2026 — Case-study post on how scientists use Claude. Built on Claude for Life Sciences (Oct 2025); Opus 4.5 shows significant improvements in figure interpretation, computational biology, protein understanding benchmarks.

**Biomni (Stanford).** General-purpose biomedical agent integrating hundreds of databases/tools/protocols. Researchers pose plain-English requests; Biomni picks resources, forms hypotheses, designs protocols, analyzes data across 25+ subfields. **GWAS for perfect pitch** — months of analysis compressed to **20 minutes**. Validated case studies: matches a 5+ year postdoc on cloning protocol design (blind eval); 450+ wearable data files from 30 people in 35 min (vs estimated 3 weeks); identified novel transcription factors in 336K-cell embryonic dataset. Custom Skills used to teach Claude expert methodology (e.g., Undiagnosed Diseases Network rare-disease diagnosis).

Other labs (Cheeseman Lab and others) build specialized systems for specific bottlenecks. Claude becoming a research collaborator across the full research process.
