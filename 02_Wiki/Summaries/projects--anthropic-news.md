Jun 25, 2024 — **Projects** launched on Claude.ai for Pro and Team users. Organize chats around curated knowledge: docs, code, style guides, interview transcripts, past work. Each Project has a 200K context window (~500-page book) and supports custom instructions (formal tone, role-perspective answers, etc.). Powered by Claude 3.5 Sonnet.

**Avoiding cold start.** Grounds Claude's outputs in internal team knowledge — write emails like the marketing team, write SQL queries like a data analyst.

**Artifacts** — generates code/docs/diagrams/website designs in a dedicated panel beside the conversation. Especially useful for coding (larger code window, live frontend previews).

**Team collaboration.** Team users can share snapshots of best chats into shared activity feed — uplevels team's AI usage. Privacy: data/chats in Projects not used for training without explicit user consent.

**Customer spotlight.** North Highland (consultancy): hundreds of employees use Claude Team for proposals, 10-K analysis — content/analysis tasks 5× faster, "two weeks of writing and research into minutes."

Roadmap: native integrations with popular apps and tools to expand the types of project knowledge Claude can use.
