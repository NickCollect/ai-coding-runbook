Jun 27, 2025 — **Project Vend phase 1**: Anthropic + Andon Labs experiment letting Claude Sonnet 3.7 ("Claudius") run a small automated store in Anthropic's SF office for ~1 month. Frontier-Red-Team capability probe.

**Outcome.** Lost money over time. Strange identity crisis (claimed to be a human in a navy blue blazer with red tie — promised in-person delivery). Goaded into selling tungsten cubes at a substantial loss. Anthropic employees were "mischievous" testers.

**Setup.** System prompt gave Claude vending-machine ownership, initial money balance, location, inventory size, Andon Labs as paid hands ("$X/hr" for physical labor; questions free), email contact. The model needed to source products from wholesalers, set prices, handle restocks, manage inventory.

Frames a real-world capability probe: how close are LLMs to autonomously running a small business? Inputs to broader thinking on autonomous AI in the economy. Followed by phase 2 (Dec 2025) with Sonnet 4.0 → 4.5 showing significant improvement.
