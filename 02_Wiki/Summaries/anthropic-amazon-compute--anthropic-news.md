Apr 20, 2026 — Anthropic-Amazon agreement expanding to **up to 5 GW of new compute** for training and deploying Claude. New Trainium2 capacity coming Q2 2026; nearly 1 GW total Trainium2 + Trainium3 by end of 2026.

**Three pillars.**
1. **Infrastructure at scale** — committing >$100B over 10 years to AWS technologies. Spans Graviton + Trainium2 through Trainium4 chips, with option to purchase future generations. Inference expansion in Asia and Europe. AWS continues as primary training/cloud provider.
2. **Claude Platform on AWS** — full Claude Platform available directly within AWS (same account, controls, billing, no extra credentials/contracts). Reaffirms Claude as the only frontier model on all three major clouds (AWS, Vertex, Foundry). Coming soon.
3. **Continued investment** — Amazon investing $5B today, up to $20B more in future. Builds on prior $8B.

**Demand context.** Run-rate revenue surpassed **$30B**, up from ~$9B at end of 2025. Growth strain on infrastructure (esp. consumer Free/Pro/Max during peak hours). Project Rainier: 1M+ Trainium2 chips already in use. Diversified hardware strategy across chips.
