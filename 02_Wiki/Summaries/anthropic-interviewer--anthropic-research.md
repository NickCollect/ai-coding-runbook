Dec 4, 2025 — **Anthropic Interviewer** — Claude-powered tool that runs detailed interviews automatically at large scale, feeding results back to human researchers. Compared to Clio (analyzing within-conversation patterns), Interviewer asks people directly about post-conversation use, feelings, future expectations.

**Initial test: 1,250 professionals.** General workforce N=1000, scientists N=125, creatives N=125. Interview data publicly released on HuggingFace with consent.

**Findings.**
- **Optimistic overall** — positive sentiment dominated. Pessimistic outliers: educational integration, artist displacement, security.
- **General workforce** wants to preserve identity-defining tasks while delegating routine work — envision shift to overseeing AI systems.
- **Creatives** use AI for productivity despite peer judgment + economic-displacement anxieties.
- **Scientists** want AI partnership but can't yet trust it for core research — confined to manuscripts and debugging.
