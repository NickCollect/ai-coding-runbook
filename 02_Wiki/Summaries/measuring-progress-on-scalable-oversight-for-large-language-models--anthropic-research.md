Nov 4, 2022 — Anthropic Alignment paper on **scalable oversight** — supervising systems that potentially outperform human supervisors. Empirical work hard since broadly-superhuman systems don't yet exist.

**Approach.** Experimental design centered on tasks where human specialists succeed but unaided humans + current general AI both fail. Proof-of-concept on MMLU and time-limited QuALITY question-answering. Found: human participants who interact with an unreliable LLM dialog assistant via chat **substantially outperform both the model alone and their own unaided performance** — encouraging baseline for scalable oversight. Foundational paper for the broader oversight research line, paired with sandwiching protocols and AI-debate work elsewhere.
