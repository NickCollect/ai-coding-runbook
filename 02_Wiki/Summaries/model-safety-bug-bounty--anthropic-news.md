Aug 8, 2024 — **Model-safety bug-bounty expansion.** New initiative focused on finding **universal jailbreaks** in unreleased safety mitigation systems. Targets high-risk domains: CBRN and cybersecurity. Bounty up to **$15,000** for novel universal jailbreaks (defined as those that consistently bypass safety across many topics, getting model to answer N specific harmful questions). Invite-only via HackerOne; plan to expand. Aligned with White House Voluntary AI Commitments (Sep 2023) and G7 Hiroshima Process Code of Conduct.
