Aug 15, 2025 — Usage Policy update (effective Sep 15, 2025).

**Key changes.**
- *Cybersecurity / agentic use* — new section explicitly outlining prohibited malicious computer/network/infrastructure-compromise activities. Continues to support consent-based vulnerability research. Help Center article on agentic use with concrete examples.
- *Political content* — softened broad prohibitions on political content; now allow legitimate policy research, civic education, political writing. Still prohibit deceptive/disruptive-to-democratic-processes uses and voter/campaign targeting.
- *Law enforcement language* — clarified previously-confusing exceptions for back-office/analytical applications.
