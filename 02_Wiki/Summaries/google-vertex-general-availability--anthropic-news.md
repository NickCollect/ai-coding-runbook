Mar 19, 2024 — **Claude 3 Haiku and Claude 3 Sonnet generally available on Google Cloud Vertex AI** (Model Garden). Enterprises can deploy Claude 3 with Google Cloud's data-residency, governance, billing, and access-permission tools. Claude 3 Opus to follow on Vertex AI in coming weeks.

Customer spotlight: **Quora's Poe app** — Claude is popular on Poe for creative writing and image understanding; millions of messages exchanged daily with Claude-based bots. Sets Vertex AI as one of three primary Claude distribution channels (alongside first-party API and Amazon Bedrock).
