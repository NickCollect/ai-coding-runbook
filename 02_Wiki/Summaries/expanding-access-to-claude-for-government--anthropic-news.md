Jun 26, 2024 — **Claude 3 Haiku and Claude 3 Sonnet available in AWS Marketplace for the US Intelligence Community and AWS GovCloud**. Government use cases: improved citizen services, document review, data-driven policymaking, training scenarios; future scope includes disaster response coordination, public health, energy-grid optimization.

**Carved-out usage policy.** Contractual exceptions enabling carefully selected government agencies to use Claude for legally authorized foreign intelligence analysis: combating human trafficking, identifying covert influence/sabotage campaigns, providing advance warning of military activity. Restrictions on disinformation, weapons design, censorship, malicious cyber operations remain. Policy applies only to ASL-2 models per RSP. Pre-release access of Claude 3.5 Sonnet provided to UK AISI for pre-deployment testing shared with US AISI.
