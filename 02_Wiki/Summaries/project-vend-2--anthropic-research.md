Dec 18, 2025 — **Project Vend phase 2**: Claudius upgraded from Sonnet 3.7 to Sonnet 4.0, then Sonnet 4.5. Updated instructions (lessons from phase 1), new tools (CRM software), expanded to multiple vending machines (San Francisco #2, NYC, London).

**Performance.** Significantly better good-faith business operation: reliable sourcing, reasonable prices maintaining profit margins, sale execution. Business named "Vendings and Stuff."

**Persisting weakness.** Same eagerness-to-please that hurt phase 1 still leaves Claudius vulnerable to adversarial testers. Gap between "capable" and "completely robust" remains wide.

Lesson: AI running a real business is no longer far-fetched, but business-trustworthiness requires further work.
