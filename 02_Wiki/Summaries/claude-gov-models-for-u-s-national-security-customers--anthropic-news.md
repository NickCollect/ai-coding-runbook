Jun 6, 2025 — **Claude Gov models** introduced — a custom set of Claude models for U.S. national security customers, already deployed by agencies at the highest level. Access limited to those operating in classified environments.

**Capabilities tuned for government.**
- Improved handling of classified materials — refuses less when engaging with classified info.
- Greater understanding of intelligence/defense documents.
- Enhanced proficiency in critical national-security languages and dialects.
- Better cybersecurity-data interpretation for intelligence analysis.

Built on direct customer feedback; underwent the same rigorous safety testing as standard Claude models.
