Oct 23, 2025 — Anthropic expanding Google Cloud TPU usage to **up to 1 million TPUs**, tens of billions in value, well over 1 GW capacity in 2026. Built on Google's seventh-generation Ironwood TPU. Customer base context: 300,000+ business customers; large accounts ($100K+ run-rate) grew nearly 7× year-over-year.

Anthropic explicitly emphasizes its **diversified compute strategy** across Google TPUs + Amazon Trainium + NVIDIA GPUs. AWS remains primary training partner; Project Rainier (massive multi-data-center cluster) continues.
