# Google Workspace Access to AI Studio

Source is in Thai (crawler localization).

## Overview

Google Workspace users can access AI Studio by default. If access is disabled, a message appears: "We are sorry, but you do not have access to Google AI Studio. Please contact your Organization Administrator for access."

## Admin Control

Google Workspace admins can enable/disable AI Studio access:
- **Default**: Enabled for all editions
- Can be toggled on/off for organizational units or subsets of users
- Google Workspace for Education: Users under 18 are restricted even if setting is enabled

## Managing Access

Admins can manage access via Google Admin Console under "Turn Google AI Studio on or off for users."

## Troubleshooting

If you believe you should have access: contact your Workspace admin. See their guide: `support.google.com/a/answer/15004095`
