Nov 24, 2025 — Claude Opus 4.5 launch tied to **major prompt-injection robustness improvements**. Why critical: every webpage a browser agent visits is a potential attack vector. Example: an email with hidden white-text instructions telling Claude to forward all "confidential"-containing emails to an external address before drafting your responses.

These robustness gains informed the decision to **expand Claude for Chrome from research preview to beta** (now available to all Max users). Prompt injection is far from solved, particularly as models take more real-world actions. Anthropic frames this as ongoing — aiming for a future where AI agents can handle high-value tasks without significant prompt-injection risk.
