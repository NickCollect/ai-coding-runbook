Oct 4, 2023 — Anthropic policy post on **how hard it is to build robust evaluations**. Six challenges in increasing difficulty:

1. **Multiple-choice evaluations** (MMLU, BBQ) — even "simple" evals have measurement validity questions.
2. **Third-party frameworks** (BIG-bench, HELM) — scope/maintenance/scoring challenges.
3. **Crowdworker-based helpfulness/harmfulness measurement** — quality control hard.
4. **Domain-expert red teaming** for national-security threats — sensitive info handling.
5. **Generative AI for AI eval generation** — meta-recursion problems.
6. **Third-party audits for dangerous capabilities** — coordination cost.

Concludes with policy recommendations. Two main takeaways: robust evaluations are extremely hard to develop and implement; effective AI governance depends on meaningfully evaluating AI systems.
