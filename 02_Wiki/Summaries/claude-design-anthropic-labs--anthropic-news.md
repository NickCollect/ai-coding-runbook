Apr 17, 2026 — **Claude Design** launched as research preview by Anthropic Labs (Pro/Max/Team/Enterprise). Powered by Opus 4.7 (most capable vision model). Conversational design tool: prototypes, slides, one-pagers, mockups, marketing collateral, frontier interactive (voice, video, shaders, 3D, built-in AI).

**How it works.**
- *Brand built in* — Claude builds a design system by reading codebase + design files during onboarding; subsequent projects use team's colors/typography/components automatically. Multiple systems supported.
- *Import from anywhere* — text prompt, images, DOCX/PPTX/XLSX, codebase, or web-capture from existing site.
- *Fine-grained refinement* — inline comments on specific elements, direct text edits, adjustment knobs for spacing/color/layout, "apply across full design" actions, custom sliders made by Claude.
- *Collaboration* — org-scoped sharing (private / link-view / link-edit), group chat with Claude.
- *Export* — internal URL, folder, Canva, PDF, PPTX, standalone HTML.
- *Handoff to Claude Code* — packages a handoff bundle with a single instruction.

**Use cases.** Designers exploring multiple directions; PMs sketching feature flows for handoff; founders/AEs producing on-brand pitch decks; marketers producing landing pages and campaign visuals; anyone building code-powered prototypes with rich interactivity.

**Customer quotes.** Canva: enables seamless flow from Claude Design drafts into Canva for further editing. Brilliant: complex pages that took 20+ prompts in other tools required only 2 prompts in Claude Design; design-intent in Claude Code handoffs makes prototype-to-production seamless.
