May 16, 2023 — **Zoom Ventures invests in Anthropic** and announces partnership. Zoom uses Claude for customer-facing AI products in Zoom Contact Center portfolio (improving end-user experience and contact-center agent performance). Zoom takes a federated AI approach (its own models + Claude + others). Early customer-facing Anthropic deployment in enterprise communications.
