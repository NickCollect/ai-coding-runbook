Jan 13, 2026 — **Anthropic Labs** expanded as a team focused on incubating experimental products at the frontier of Claude's capabilities. Mike Krieger (Instagram co-founder, Anthropic CPO for two years) joins Labs alongside Ben Mann. **Ami Vora** to lead the Product organization (joined Anthropic late 2025), partnering with CTO Rahul Patil.

Labs has produced (or contributed to): **Claude Code** ($1B in 6 months), **MCP** (100M monthly downloads, industry standard), **Skills**, **Claude in Chrome**, **Cowork** (research preview launched the day before — desktop agentic capabilities). Focus: tinkering at the edge → testing unpolished versions with early users → scaling what lands. Daniela Amodei: "Labs gives us room to break the mold and explore."
