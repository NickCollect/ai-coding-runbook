Jun 21, 2024 — Claude 3.5 Sonnet launched as the first release of the Claude 3.5 family. Outperforms competitors and Claude 3 Opus across evaluations at the speed and cost of mid-tier Sonnet. Free on Claude.ai and iOS app; Pro/Team plans get higher rate limits. $3/$15 per M tokens, 200K context.

**Performance.**
- Industry benchmarks: GPQA (graduate reasoning), MMLU (undergrad knowledge), HumanEval (coding) all at SOTA.
- 2× speed of Claude 3 Opus.
- Internal agentic-coding eval: 64% (vs Opus 38%) — fix bug or add functionality to open-source codebase from natural-language description. With tools, can independently write, edit, execute code with reasoning + troubleshooting. Particularly effective for legacy app updates and codebase migrations.
- SOTA vision: surpasses Claude 3 Opus on standard benchmarks. Notable for chart/graph interpretation and OCR from imperfect images (retail, logistics, financial services).

**Artifacts launched alongside the model** — preview feature on Claude.ai. Generated content (code, text docs, website designs) appears in a dedicated window beside the conversation; users can see, edit, build upon. Marks Claude's evolution from conversational AI to collaborative work environment. Future direction: team collaboration, organization-wide shared spaces with Claude as on-demand teammate.

**Safety.** ASL-2. Pre-deployment safety evaluation by UK AISI shared with US AISI under MoU. Engaged child-safety experts at Thorn for classifier updates. Anthropic does not train generative models on user-submitted data without explicit permission.

**Roadmap.** Claude 3.5 Haiku and Claude 3.5 Opus planned later in 2024 (3.5 Opus was eventually skipped — direct jump to Claude 4 later). Memory feature mentioned as upcoming exploration.
