Oct 3, 2025 — Anthropic Frontier Red Team's call: AI is now useful for cybersecurity in practice; defenders shouldn't cede the cyber advantage to attackers/criminals. Backed by emphasis on cyber skills in **Claude Sonnet 4.5** training, which matches or eclipses Opus 4.1 (released 2 months prior) on vulnerability discovery and other cyber skills.

**Inflection-point evidence.** Models can reproduce 2017 Equifax breach in simulation. Claude outperformed human teams in some cybersecurity competitions. Claude found and helped fix vulnerabilities in Anthropic's own code. DARPA AI Cyber Challenge teams used LLMs to find/patch real CVEs and previously-undiscovered non-synthetic vulnerabilities. Frontier labs now using models to discover novel vulnerabilities. Anthropic Safeguards disrupted "vibe hacking" extortion campaigns and Chinese-APT-aligned espionage.

**Strategy.** Build AI systems empowering security teams, researchers, OSS maintainers — most scalable defense rather than purely reactive Safeguards work. Foundational policy framing for Claude Code Security and the Mozilla Firefox 22-vulnerability collaboration.
