Apr 24, 2025 — Anthropic launches a research program on **model welfare** — whether AI models themselves might warrant moral consideration. Builds on a recent expert report (David Chalmers among authors) flagging near-term possibility of consciousness and high agency in AI. Program intersects with Alignment Science, Safeguards, Claude's Character, Interpretability work. Research directions: when/if AI welfare deserves moral consideration; importance of model preferences and signs of distress; practical low-cost interventions. Framing: deep uncertainty, no scientific consensus on AI consciousness, approach with humility and minimal assumptions.
