May 10, 2024 — Usage Policy update (effective Jun 6, 2024). Renamed from "Acceptable Use Policy" → "Usage Policy". Consolidated previously-split "Prohibited Uses" + "Prohibited Business Cases" into single "Universal Usage Standards" applicable to both businesses and consumers.

**Major changes.**
- *Election integrity / misinformation* — explicit prohibitions: no promoting candidate/party/issue/position, no soliciting votes/contributions, no interfering with elections (targeting voting machines, obstructing counting), no false election-law content.
- *High-risk use cases* — healthcare decisions and legal guidance integrations require additional safety measures.
- *Minors* — Consumer Terms still 18+, but organizations can integrate API in products for minors with safety features and AI disclosure.
- *Law enforcement* — expanded country list for tailored use cases (call-center support, document summarization).
