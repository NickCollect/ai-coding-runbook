Sep 19, 2023 — **Original Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP)** published. AI Safety Level (ASL) framework loosely modeled on US biosafety levels (BSL):
- **ASL-1** — no meaningful catastrophic risk (chess bot, 2018-era LLM).
- **ASL-2** — early signs of dangerous capabilities (e.g., bioweapon-build instructions) but not useful in practice. Current LLMs including Claude assessed as ASL-2.
- **ASL-3** — substantially increases catastrophic-misuse risk vs non-AI baselines, OR shows low-level autonomous capabilities. Requires unusually strong security and commitment not to deploy if any meaningful catastrophic-misuse risk under adversarial red-teaming.
- **ASL-4+** — undefined initially; later may require unsolved-research-problem safety methods like interpretability-based behavior demonstrations.

Goal: race-to-the-top dynamic across frontier labs. Approved by Anthropic board; changes require Long Term Benefit Trust consultation. Acknowledged as best-current-guess that will iterate.
