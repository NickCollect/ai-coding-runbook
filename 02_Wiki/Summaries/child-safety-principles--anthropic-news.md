Apr 23, 2024 — Anthropic joined other leading AI companies in **Safety by Design** principles led by **Thorn** and **All Tech Is Human** to mitigate AI-generated child sexual abuse material (AIG-CSAM) and child sexual exploitation material (CSEM).

**Anthropic commitments.**
- *Develop* — responsibly source training data avoiding known CSAM/CSEM risks; detect/remove/report CSAM/CSEM at ingestion; structured red-teaming for AIG-CSAM/CSEM; specific training-data and model-development policies; prohibit customer use for sexual harm to children.
- *Deploy* — usage policies prohibiting child sexual exploitation, NCMEC reporting, multimodal output limitations.

Anthropic models do not have multimodal outputs (only ingest images), reducing AIG-CSAM risk vector.
