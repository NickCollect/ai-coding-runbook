Mar 14, 2023 — **Claude debuts publicly** after months in closed alpha with partners (Notion, Quora, DuckDuckGo). Two versions: Claude (high-performance) and Claude Instant (lighter, cheaper, faster). Accessible via chat interface and API in developer console.

**Positioning.** Next-generation AI assistant grounded in Anthropic's helpful/honest/harmless research. Conversational and text-processing tasks: summarization, search, creative/collaborative writing, Q&A, coding. Customers report fewer harmful outputs, easier conversation, more steerable. Takes direction on personality, tone, behavior.

**Launch partners.**
- **Quora** offered Claude through Poe AI Chat. User feedback: "more conversational than ChatGPT," "more interactive and creative in storytelling."
- **Juni Learning** powering Discord Juni Tutor Bot — chose Claude for richer subject-matter answers.
- **Notion** — Claude powers Notion AI for writing/summarization within Notion workspace.
- **DuckDuckGo DuckAssist** — first NL Instant Answer feature in DuckDuckGo search results, generating answers from Wikipedia and related sources.
- **Robin AI** (legal contract evaluation) — "nothing has matched Claude's capabilities" (vs prior 2019-onward AI work).
- **AssemblyAI** — audio transcription / Conversation Intelligence platform.

The launch marked the beginning of Anthropic's commercial product trajectory.
