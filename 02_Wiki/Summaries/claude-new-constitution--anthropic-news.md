Jan 22, 2026 — **New Claude constitution published**, replacing the original 2023 standalone-principles list. Released under CC0 1.0 — free for any use. Constitution serves as foundational training document treated as final authority on Claude's behavior; published for transparency.

**Approach shift.** Previous constitution was standalone principles. New version explains *why* — believing that for AI to act well across novel situations, it needs to understand reasoning behind values, not just follow rules. Specific rules ("hard constraints") still used for very high-stakes behaviors Claude should never engage in, but most guidance is principle-based and intended to be generalizable.

**Function in training.** Used at multiple training stages (evolving from 2023 Constitutional AI). Claude itself uses the constitution to construct synthetic training data — conversations, responses, rankings — that train future versions to embody it. Practical artifact + statement of ideals.

**Goal.** Claude as a coherent entity with good values it understands, rather than a rule-follower that mechanically applies opaque guidelines.
