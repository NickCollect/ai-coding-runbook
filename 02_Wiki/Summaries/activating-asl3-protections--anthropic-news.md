May 22, 2025 — **AI Safety Level 3 (ASL-3) Deployment and Security Standards activated** alongside Claude Opus 4 launch. Precautionary measure under the RSP — Anthropic hasn't definitively determined Opus 4 has crossed the Capability Threshold but cannot conclusively rule it out (ASL-4 ruled out; Sonnet 4 ASL-3 ruled out).

**ASL-3 deployment measures.** Narrowly focused on preventing CBRN-weapons assistance — specifically end-to-end CBRN workflows that are additive over no-LLM baselines. Includes defenses against universal jailbreaks. Does NOT aim to address single common-knowledge questions ("What is the chemical formula for sarin?") or non-CBRN issues.

**ASL-3 security measures.** Increased internal security to make model-weight theft harder; suitable against sophisticated non-state attackers.

Rationale: easier to develop/test/refine protections before strictly required, simplifies model releases. May remove ASL-3 if Opus 4 evaluation later shows capability is below threshold.
