# Gemini API Available Regions

Source is in Arabic (crawler localization).

## Overview

Google AI Studio and Gemini API are available in many countries/regions. If unable to access, possible reasons: regional restriction, under 18, account not age-verified.

## Why Access May Be Blocked

- **Regional restrictions**: AI Studio not available in your region
- **Age**: Must be 18+ to access
- **Account verification**: Age not yet verified on Google account

## Availability

Available in most countries worldwide including: Albania, Algeria, American Samoa, Andorra, Angola, Argentina, Armenia, Australia, Austria, Azerbaijan, Bahamas, Bahrain, Bangladesh, Barbados, Belgium, Belize, Benin, Bermuda, Bhutan, Bolivia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Botswana, Brazil, Brunei, and hundreds more countries.

**Not available** in: China, Russia, and certain other countries under sanctions/restrictions (not explicitly listed in source).

## Alternative for Unavailable Regions

Use the Gemini API via **Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform** on Google Cloud, which may have different regional availability.

## Notes

- Google AI Studio usage is free in all available regions.
- For detailed/complete country list, see the official page at the source URL.
