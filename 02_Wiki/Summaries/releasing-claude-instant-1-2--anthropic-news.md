Aug 9, 2023 — **Claude Instant 1.2** released via the API. Faster, lower-priced model that incorporates Claude 2's strengths: improved math, coding, reasoning, safety; longer/more structured responses; better formatting-instruction following; better quote extraction, multilingual, Q&A.

**Benchmarks.**
- Codex (coding): 58.7% (up from 52.8%).
- GSM8K (math): 86.7% (up from 80.9%).
- Hallucinates less; more resistant to jailbreaks per automated red-teaming.

Aimed at casual dialogue, text analysis, summarization, document comprehension where speed and cost matter more than peak intelligence.
