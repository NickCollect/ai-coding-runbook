Dec 2, 2025 — **Claude for Nonprofits** launched in partnership with GivingTuesday. Three components: discounted access (up to 75% off Team and Enterprise), connectors to nonprofit-specific tools (Blackbaud, Candid, Benevity), free *AI Fluency for Nonprofits* course.

**Discounted plan access.** Includes Opus 4.6, Sonnet 4.5, Haiku 4.5.

**New connectors.**
- *Benevity* — 2.4M+ validated nonprofits for volunteering/donation searches.
- *Blackbaud* — CRM and fundraising for donor management, campaign tracking.
- *Candid* — nonprofit/funder data for grants and philanthropy.

**Existing nonprofit users.** Epilepsy Foundation (24/7 support to 3.4M Americans with epilepsy via Claude). International Rescue Committee (faster field-data analysis in humanitarian settings). IDinsight (16× faster work with Claude). SkillUp, Robin Hood (coding/admin offload).
