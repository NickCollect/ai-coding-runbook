Feb 26, 2025 — **Claude models help power Alexa+** as part of the Amazon-Anthropic partnership. Anthropic's CPO Mike Krieger led the joint team. Alexa+ accesses Claude through Amazon Bedrock; rollout begins in the US in coming weeks. Notable: a consumer voice assistant deployment giving Claude reach into Amazon's Echo install base, leveraging Claude's "best in class jailbreaking resistance" for consumer safety.
