Jul 18, 2023 — Companion paper to measuring-faithfulness. Approach: have models generate reasoning by **decomposing questions into subquestions answered in separate contexts**. Decomposition-based methods achieve strong QA performance, sometimes approaching CoT, while improving the faithfulness of model-generated reasoning. Forcing the model to answer simpler subquestions in separate contexts greatly increases faithfulness over CoT, while still capturing some performance gains. Evidence that faithfulness is improvable; a path toward verification-friendly model-generated reasoning.
