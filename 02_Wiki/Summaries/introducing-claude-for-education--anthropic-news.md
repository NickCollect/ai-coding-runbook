Apr 2, 2025 — **Claude for Education** launched as specialized Claude for higher education institutions. Four pillars:

1. **Learning mode** — guides reasoning rather than providing answers ("How would you approach this?"); develops critical thinking; works within Projects.
2. **University-wide availability** — full campus agreements with Northeastern, LSE, Champlain College.
3. **Academic partnerships** — joining Internet2, Instructure (Canvas LMS embedding).
4. **Student programs** — Campus Ambassadors program, API credits for student projects.

Use cases: students draft literature reviews with citations, work through calculus step-by-step, get thesis feedback. Faculty: rubrics aligned to learning outcomes, individualized essay feedback. Admin: enrollment trend analysis, automating email responses, converting policy docs to FAQ.
