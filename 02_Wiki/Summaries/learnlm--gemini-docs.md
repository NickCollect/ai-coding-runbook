# LearnLM

Source is in Hindi (crawler localization).

## Status

**LearnLM is no longer available as a separate model in AI Studio.**

LearnLM's capabilities have been integrated directly into Gemini — starting with the Gemini 2.5 model series.

## Where to Find LearnLM Capabilities

For current LearnLM documentation: `cloud.google.com/solutions/learnlm`

LearnLM features are now part of standard Gemini 2.5+ models. No separate model selection needed.

## Historical Note

LearnLM was Google's research initiative to apply learning science principles to AI models for educational use cases. Capabilities include adaptive pedagogy, encouraging metacognition, personalizing learning, and managing cognitive load.
