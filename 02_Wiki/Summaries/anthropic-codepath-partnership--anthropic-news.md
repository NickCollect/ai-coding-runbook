Feb 13, 2026 — **CodePath partnership** putting Claude and Claude Code at the center of CodePath's coding curriculum and career programs. Reach: **20,000+ students** at community colleges, state schools, HBCUs (40%+ from families <$50K/year). Claude integrated into Foundations of AI Engineering, Applications of AI Engineering, AI Open-Source Capstone courses. Students contribute to real OSS projects (GitLab, Puter, Dokploy). Howard University's redesigned Intro to AI course (with CodePath + Thurgood Marshall College Fund) is the first time CodePath's applied AI curriculum is offered for academic credit.
