Feb 2, 2026 — Two flagship life-sciences partnerships.

**HHMI (AI@HHMI initiative).** Anchored at Janelia Research Campus. Develop specialized AI agents for use within labs — comprehensive source of experimental knowledge integrated with cutting-edge instruments and analysis pipelines. Long-term close collaboration on model deployment + ongoing development.

**Allen Institute.** Multi-agent AI systems for multi-modal data analysis: multi-omic data integration, knowledge graph management, temporal dynamics modeling, experimental design — coordinated to support full arc of scientific investigation.

Both partnerships emphasize transparency, interpretability, and Claude as augment-not-replace for human scientific judgment.
