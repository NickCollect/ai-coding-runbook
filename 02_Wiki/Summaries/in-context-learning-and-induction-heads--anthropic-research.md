Mar 8, 2022 — Foundational interpretability paper from Anthropic's Transformer Circuits research line. Introduces **induction heads** — attention heads whose mechanism explains a substantial fraction of in-context learning in transformer language models. Influential reference for understanding emergent capabilities and the broader mechanistic interpretability program; cited extensively in subsequent dictionary-learning, sparse-autoencoder, and circuits work.
