Sep 25, 2023 — **Amazon to invest up to $4B in Anthropic**. AWS becomes Anthropic's primary cloud provider for mission-critical workloads. Access to AWS Trainium and Inferentia chips for training and deployment. Expanded Amazon Bedrock support including secure model customization and fine-tuning.

Customer examples: LexisNexis Lexis+ AI uses fine-tuned Claude 2; Bridgewater Associates building Claude 2-powered investment-analyst assistant; Lonely Planet reduced itinerary-generation costs ~80% with Claude 2. Foundational moment for the Anthropic-AWS deep collaboration; later expanded to $8B (Nov 2024) and ~$33B (Apr 2026 Amazon-compute deal).
