Oct 15, 2024 — **Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP) v2** published. Inspired by safety-case methodologies (Bloomfield/Rushby). Two-key-component framework: **Capability Thresholds** (specific AI abilities that, if reached, require stronger safeguards) and **Required Safeguards** (the ASL Standards needed to mitigate risks once a threshold is reached).

**Two defined Capability Thresholds:**
- *Autonomous AI R&D* — model independently conducting complex AI research → require ASL-4+ (security elevated to prevent uncontrolled acceleration).
- *CBRN weapons* — model can meaningfully assist a basic-technical-background person creating/deploying CBRN weapons → ASL-3.

All current models operate under ASL-2 (industry best practices).
