Feb 15, 2022 — Early Anthropic Societal Impacts policy paper. Highlights counterintuitive property of large generative models (GPT-3, Megatron-Turing NLG, Gopher etc.): **predictable loss** on broad training distributions (scaling laws) combined with **unpredictable specific capabilities, inputs, and outputs**.

High-level predictability + appearance of useful capabilities drives rapid development. Unpredictable qualities make it difficult to anticipate deployment consequences → can lead to socially harmful behavior. Policy-oriented analysis: examines how these properties motivate developers' deployment decisions, identifies challenges that hinder deployment, lists possible interventions for beneficial impact. Foundational policy framing for Anthropic's later RSP and related governance work.
