Oct 20, 2025 — **Claude for Life Sciences** announced — supports the entire research lifecycle from early discovery through translation and commercialization.

**Model improvements.** Sonnet 4.5 vs prior on:
- *Protocol QA* (lab-protocol understanding) — 0.83 (human baseline 0.79; Sonnet 4: 0.74).
- *BixBench* (bioinformatics) — similar improvement over predecessor.

**Connectors added.**
- *Benchling* — questions linked back to source experiments, notebooks, records.
- *BioRender* — vetted scientific figures, icons, templates.
- *PubMed* — millions of biomedical papers and clinical studies.
- *Wiley Scholar Gateway* — peer-reviewed scientific content.
- *Synapse.org* — collaborative public/private project data sharing.
- *10x Genomics* — single-cell and spatial analysis in natural language.

Plus existing general-purpose connectors (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365 SharePoint/OneDrive/Outlook/Teams, Databricks for bioinformatics analytics, Snowflake NL search). **Agent Skills** support added for life sciences. Life-sciences-specific prompt library and dedicated support also rolled out.

Anthropic frames this as part of its public-benefit mission to accelerate scientific discovery — eventually toward AI models making discoveries autonomously.
