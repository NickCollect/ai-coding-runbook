Jul 25, 2023 — Cybersecurity-best-practices manifesto for frontier AI labs.

**Core recommendation: two-party (multi-party) authorization** for AI-critical infrastructure. No person has persistent access to production-critical envs; each request needs a coworker's time-limited approval with business justification. Pattern from secure vaults / GMP / ISO 9001 / ISO 22000 / ISO 13485 / SOX.

**Standards to adopt.** NIST Secure Software Development Framework (SSDF), Supply Chain Levels for Software Artifacts (SLSA). Translatable from general software dev to model dev/deploy. EO 14028 already drove SSDF adoption for federal contractors.

**Posture.** Frontier AI sector should be treated as critical infrastructure with public-private partnership in securing models and the companies developing them. Voluntary at first; eventually procurement/regulatory mandates appropriate.
