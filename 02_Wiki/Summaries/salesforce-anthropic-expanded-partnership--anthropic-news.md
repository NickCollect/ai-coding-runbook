Oct 14, 2025 — Expanded Anthropic-Salesforce partnership.

**Three initiatives.**
1. **Claude as preferred foundation model in Agentforce** for regulated industries (financial services, healthcare, cybersecurity, life sciences) via Amazon Bedrock. Anthropic is **the first LLM provider fully integrated within Salesforce trust boundary** — all Claude traffic stays in Salesforce VPC. Customers: CrowdStrike, RBC Wealth Management.
2. **Industry-specific AI solutions** — starting with Claude for Financial Services + Agentforce Financial Services for AI agents understanding financial instruments, insurance claims, industry frameworks.
3. **Deeper Slack integration** — Slack MCP server lets Claude access channels/messages/files for summaries, decisions, drafts. Invoke Claude in Slack to analyze docs, perform web searches, pull from Salesforce CRM/Tableau/etc.

Salesforce also deploying Claude Code across global engineering org. Anthropic broadening internal Slack use.
