Feb 25, 2026 — **Anthropic acquired Vercept** to push computer-use capabilities. Vercept co-founders Kiana Ehsani, Luca Weihs, Ross Girshick spent years on perception and interaction problems — directly relevant to Anthropic's hardest problems. Vercept's external product winding down. Acquisition follows Sonnet 4.6 launch (OSWorld score 72.5%, up from <15% in late 2024). Sonnet 4.6 approaches human-level on tasks like complex spreadsheets and cross-tab web forms. Second team-acquisition in the recent stretch following Bun.
