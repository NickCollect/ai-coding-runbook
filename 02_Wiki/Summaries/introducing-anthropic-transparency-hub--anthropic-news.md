Feb 27, 2025 — **Transparency Hub** launched at anthropic.com/transparency. First periodic report covers: banned accounts, account appeals, appeal overturns, NCMEC reports, government request data. Hub also covers methodologies for model evals/safety testing, abuse detection/enforcement, internal governance and risk-assessment policies, societal-impact methods, AI-safety research initiatives, security and privacy safeguards. Designed as a unified framework addressing the spectrum of transparency-initiative requirements. Periodic reporting commitment.
