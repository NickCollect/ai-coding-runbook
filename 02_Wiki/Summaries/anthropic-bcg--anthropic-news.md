Sep 14, 2023 — **Boston Consulting Group (BCG) partnership**. BCG customers worldwide get direct access to Claude for strategic AI offerings. BCG advises on Anthropic-model deployment (Claude 2 etc.) for knowledge management, market research, fraud detection, demand forecasting, report generation, business analysis. BCG also uses Claude internally. Among the first major management-consultancy partnerships for Anthropic.
