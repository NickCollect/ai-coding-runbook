Apr 3, 2025 — **Code with Claude** announced — Anthropic's first developer conference. May 22, 2025 at The Midway in San Francisco. Hands-on, one-day event focused on real-world implementations and best practices using Anthropic API, CLI tools, and MCP. Open to a select group of developers and founders by application.

Programming includes: Anthropic exec/product team talks, interactive workshops/labs, technical-team office hours, community networking. Topics: product roadmap, Claude Code, MCP, dev methodologies, AI agent implementation, tool-use patterns. The May 22 date coincides with the **Claude 4 launch** (Opus 4 / Sonnet 4) — making the conference Claude 4's public-launch venue.
