Jul 11, 2023 — Claude 2 launched. Available via API and the new public beta site **claude.ai** (US/UK). Improved performance, longer responses, longer memory.

**Headline numbers.** Bar exam multiple choice 76.5% (up from 73.0% on Claude 1.3). GRE reading + writing >90th percentile vs. grad-school applicants. Codex HumanEval (Python) 71.2% (up from 56.0%). GSM8K (grade-school math) 88.0% (up from 85.2%).

**Context window.** Up to **100K tokens** input; can produce multi-thousand-token outputs in one go.

**Safety.** 2× more harmless responses than Claude 1.3 in internal red-teaming. Constitutional AI + extensive red-teaming techniques applied.

**Pricing.** Same as Claude 1.3. Partner highlights: Jasper (long-form low-latency content), Sourcegraph Cody (codebase Q&A with 100K context). The launch also marked the broader public availability of Claude beyond closed alpha.
