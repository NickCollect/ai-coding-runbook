Aug 12, 2025 — Overview of the **Anthropic Safeguards team** and its approach across the model lifecycle: policy, training influence, harmful-output testing, real-time enforcement, novel-misuse detection.

**Two policy mechanisms.**
- *Unified Harm Framework* — five dimensions (physical, psychological, economic, societal, individual autonomy) used as a structured lens (not formal grading) for likelihood + scale of misuse.
- *Policy Vulnerability Testing* — partnerships with external domain experts (terrorism, radicalization, child safety, mental health) stress-test policies. Example: 2024 US election partnership with Institute for Strategic Dialogue led to authoritative-source banner (TurboVote) for election questions.

**Training collaboration.** Safeguards works with fine-tuning teams to define what behaviors Claude should/shouldn't exhibit; informs trait selection during training. Plus real-time enforcement classifiers, novel-misuse intelligence work tied to Threat Intelligence reports.
